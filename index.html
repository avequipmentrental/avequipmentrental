Why Choose LED Video Walls?

LED walls consist of multiple panels that work together to create a seamless, giant display. Unlike projectors, they provide deep blacks, vibrant colors, and enough brightness to be seen clearly even in broad daylight or under heavy stage lighting.

Types of LED Screens Available

Depending on your venue and audience distance, different types of LED screens are used:

  • Indoor LED Walls: Featuring fine pixel pitches (e.g., 2.5mm or 1.9mm) for crisp visuals even when viewed from a short distance. Perfect for corporate seminars.
  • Outdoor LED Walls: Built with high-brightness components and weather-resistant technology, ideal for festivals and outdoor screenings.
  • Curved & Creative Displays: Modular panels that can be configured into curves or unique shapes to fit creative stage designs.
